  <p>
    <strong>
      Lofoten is a very special place to us and you’ve travelled long and hard to
      get here, so we want to make sure you get to interact with it in the short
      time that you’re here.
    </strong>
  </p>

  <p>
    <strong>
      Like any good summer camp, we’re taking you on some fun adventures — please
      pick your first & second favourite in order of preference.
    </strong>
  </p>

  <p><strong><u>1. Fishing</u></strong></p>
  <p>
    <strong>
      Henningsvær is a fishing village both historically and to this day! We’ll
      take you on a boat with local fishermen that will teach you the ropes of
      their trades. Anything you catch you get to keep!
      <br>
      <em>Maybe add iconic pic of Jack</em>
    </strong>
  </p>

  <p><strong><u>2. Hiking Festvågtind</u></strong></p>
  <p>
    <strong>
      Right across the bridge connecting Henningsvær to the other islands, there
      is a beautiful hike that will take you 2–4 hours depending if you go all
      the way to the top or not — there’s an opportunity to stop halfway and the
      views are just as beautiful!). The views are stunning and we’ll have guides
      helping you navigate the trail.
      <br>
      Please note this is a steep hike, where you’ll need to use your hands for
      stability, so things get athletic, but the view of the town you get from
      the halfway point alone is worth it!
    </strong>
  </p>

  <p><strong><u>3. Climbing</u></strong></p>
  <p>
    <strong>
      Lofoten is a very special place for climbing, and climbing is a very special
      activity for us.
      <br>
      We want to give the opportunity to try climbing on real Lofoten rock to
      those of you who’s been wanting to try climbing outdoors or just curious
      about the whole thing!
      <br>
      We’ll have guides there with equipment to borrow & pre-set ropes — so this
      is open to complete beginners as well as anyone who’s already tried climbing
      in a gym.
      <br>
      If you have your own shoes or harness — bring them, but otherwise we have
      you covered.
    </strong>
  </p>

  <p>
    <strong>
      <u>4. Sauna + beer at Trevarefabrikken with an option of Yoga class</u>
    </strong>
  </p>
  <p>
    <strong>
      Trevarefabrikken has a sauna with a pretty insane sea view that’s ours to
      use the entire weekend, so if your perfect activity involves getting very
      hot and then cooling down with a beer, you’re welcome to stay at
      Trevarefabrikken and do just that!
      <br>
      We’ll also have a guided yoga class at 11.00 — if you’re choosing this
      activity option, please let us know if you’d like to join the yoga too!
    </strong>
  </p>
